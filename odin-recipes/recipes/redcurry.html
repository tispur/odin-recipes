Red Curry

This recipe is easy and delicious!

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • kosher salt
  • 2 shallots
  • 3 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 bunch broccolini
  • 1 lime
